Resourcing

Posted on November 8th, 2021

Part of Notes on Project Management

A resource is a necessary asset which functions to (help) complete a specific task(s) or project.

  • Person/Team
  • Tools
    • Software (plus licensing)
    • Hardware (plus fees)
  • Equipment
    • Premises
    • Office space
    • Chairs, tables
  • Services (3rd parties to support in-house resources)
    • Domain specialists
    • Technology specialists
    • Cloud hosting
    • Additional team members (pad em out)

When deducing a resource, consider a 'full-time employee' whose actual work corresponds to ~170 working days per year (or 3.27 days per week).
